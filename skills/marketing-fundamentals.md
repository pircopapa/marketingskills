# Marketing Fundamentals

Core marketing concepts and frameworks every marketer should know.

## Skill Overview

- **Category**: Foundation
- **Level**: Beginner
- **Time to Learn**: 2-4 weeks
- **Prerequisites**: None

## What You'll Learn

Understanding the fundamental principles that drive effective marketing strategies across all channels and industries.

## Core Concepts

### 1. The 4 Ps of Marketing

- **Product**: What you're selling and how it meets customer needs
- **Price**: Pricing strategy and perceived value
- **Place**: Distribution channels and where customers find you
- **Promotion**: How you communicate your value proposition

### 2. Target Audience & Buyer Personas

Defining who your ideal customer is:
- Demographics (age, location, income)
- Psychographics (values, interests, lifestyle)
- Pain points and motivations
- Buying behavior and decision triggers

### 3. Value Proposition

A clear statement that explains:
- How your product solves a problem
- The specific benefits it delivers
- Why customers should choose you over competitors

### 4. The Marketing Funnel

```
Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Intent → Purchase → Loyalty
```

Each stage requires different messaging and tactics.

### 5. Brand Positioning

How your brand occupies a distinct place in the mind of your target customer relative to competitors.

## Key Frameworks

### STP Model
- **Segmentation**: Divide the market into distinct groups
- **Targeting**: Select which segments to focus on
- **Positioning**: Define how to position your offering

### SWOT Analysis
- **Strengths**: Internal advantages
- **Weaknesses**: Internal limitations
- **Opportunities**: External factors to leverage
- **Threats**: External challenges to address

## Practical Exercises

1. Write a value proposition for a product you use daily
2. Create a basic buyer persona for a hypothetical business
3. Map a customer journey through the marketing funnel
4. Conduct a SWOT analysis for a brand you admire
